Referral Program Terms

Effective date: May 13, 2026  •  Last updated: May 13, 2026

1. Program Overview

The Nexus Referral Program ("Program") allows active Nexus clients to earn account credits by referring new clients to Nexus ZC LLC. We believe in rewarding the trust our clients place in us by sharing their experience with others.

By participating in the Program, you agree to these Referral Program Terms, which are incorporated into and subject to our Terms of Service.

2. Eligibility

To participate in the Nexus Referral Program, you must:

  • Be an active, paying Nexus client with a current service agreement in effect
  • Have an account in good standing — your account must be current with no overdue balances
  • Be 18 years of age or older
  • Be referring individuals or businesses that are not existing Nexus clients and have not previously been in contact with Nexus ZC LLC as a prospect

Employees, contractors, and principals of Nexus ZC LLC are not eligible to participate in the Program. The Program is limited to US-based businesses at this time.

3. How to Refer

Once you are confirmed as an eligible referral participant, you will receive a unique referral link associated with your account. To make a valid referral:

  • Share your unique referral link with the person or business you are referring
  • The referred party must click your link and complete their intake or sign-up within 30 days
  • The referred party must become an active paying client (complete their first paid invoice)
  • Self-referrals — using your own referral link — are not eligible and will be disqualified

Contact zach@nexuszc.com to request your referral link if you have not already received one.

Credits are applied to the referring client's account after the referred client's first invoice is paid in full. There is no limit on the number of referrals you may make, subject to the eligibility and fraud provisions of these Terms.

5. Credit Terms and Conditions

  • Referral rewards are issued as account credits only — they have no cash value and cannot be exchanged for cash, check, wire transfer, or any other form of payment.
  • Credits are non-transferable and may only be applied to your Nexus account.
  • Credits expire 12 months from the date of issuance if not applied.
  • Credits are forfeited upon account termination, except as required by applicable law.
  • Multiple referrals to the same business or person are counted as a single referral.

6. Tax Reporting

Nexus ZC LLC will issue a Form 1099-NEC to any participant whose accumulated referral rewards exceed $600 in a single calendar year, as required by the Internal Revenue Service. You are responsible for reporting all referral rewards as income on your tax returns, regardless of whether a 1099 is issued.

By participating in the Program, you agree to provide accurate tax identification information (name, address, and Social Security Number or EIN) upon request. Failure to provide this information may result in suspension of your referral participation and/or backup withholding.

7. Prohibited Conduct

The following activities are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disqualification from the Program and forfeiture of all pending and accrued credits:

  • Self-referrals or referring fictitious individuals or businesses
  • Creating multiple accounts to exploit the Program
  • Using spam, unsolicited mass emails, or deceptive advertising to generate referrals
  • Misrepresenting Nexus ZC LLC's services, pricing, or capabilities to referred parties
  • Any form of fraud, manipulation, or abuse of the referral system
  • Referring parties who previously declined Nexus services or were disqualified by Nexus

8. Nexus ZC LLC's Discretion

Nexus ZC LLC reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to:

  • Disqualify any referral that does not meet Program requirements
  • Investigate suspected fraud or abuse
  • Suspend or terminate a participant's access to the Program at any time
  • Reverse previously issued credits if fraud is later discovered
  • Determine whether a referred party qualifies for a particular plan tier

Nexus ZC LLC's determinations regarding referral eligibility and reward amounts are final.

9. Program Modifications

Nexus ZC LLC reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate the Referral Program at any time with 30 days written notice to current participants. Changes to reward amounts or eligibility criteria will not affect credits already earned and posted to your account prior to the effective date of the change.
