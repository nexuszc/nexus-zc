Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 13, 2026  •  Last updated: May 13, 2026

1. Who We Are

Nexus ZC LLC ("Nexus," "we," "us," or "our") is a Colorado limited liability company. Our principal place of business is 2812 11th Street, Boulder CO 80304. You can reach us at zach@nexuszc.com.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at nexuszc.com, our AI diagnostic services, our client portal, or any other products or services we offer (collectively, the "Services").

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Intake form data: Your name, business name, business URL, email address, phone number, industry, and answers to diagnostic questions you provide when submitting a request for a Nexus Score or diagnostic.
  • Publicly available web data: When you request an AI diagnostic, we may retrieve publicly available information about your business — such as your website content, Google Business Profile data, and publicly visible social media — solely to perform the diagnostic.
  • Usage analytics: We collect information about how you interact with our Services, including page views, form submissions, time on page, and report opens. This data is used in aggregate to improve the product.
  • Communications: If you email or message us, we retain those communications to provide support and context for your account.
  • Device and log data: Standard server logs including IP address, browser type, operating system, and referral URL. We do not sell or share this data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Run AI-powered business diagnostics and generate your Nexus Score and report
  • Communicate your diagnostic results via email and/or SMS (based on your consent)
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of our AI models and diagnostic methodology
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send operational emails related to your account or diagnostics
  • Send marketing communications (only with your explicit consent, and you may unsubscribe at any time)
  • Detect fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service

4. AI Disclosure

Nexus diagnostics — including your Nexus Score, identified gaps, revenue leakage estimates, quick wins, and 90-day roadmap — are generated using artificial intelligence (AI) technology, including large language models. These outputs are produced algorithmically based on the information you provide and publicly available data about your business.

AI-generated diagnostics are for informational purposes only. They do not constitute legal, financial, tax, or professional business advice. Nexus ZC LLC makes no guarantees of accuracy, completeness, or fitness for any particular purpose. Always consult qualified professionals before making significant business decisions.

5. Data Retention

  • Diagnostic data (non-clients): Retained for 2 years from the date of submission, then deleted or anonymized.
  • Active client data: Retained for the duration of your contract plus 1 year after termination, then deleted unless required by law.
  • Communications: Retained for 3 years from last contact.
  • Usage analytics: Retained indefinitely in anonymized, aggregated form.

6. Who Sees Your Data

Your personal and business information is accessed only by Zach Curtis, founder of Nexus ZC LLC, and our AI processing infrastructure. We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your data with any third party for their marketing or commercial purposes.

We may share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use trusted third-party infrastructure providers (including Supabase for database hosting, Resend for email delivery, Twilio for SMS, OpenAI for AI processing) under strict data processing agreements. They process data only on our behalf and may not use it for their own purposes.
  • Legal obligations: If required by law, court order, or legitimate government request, we may disclose information as necessary.
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you in advance.

7. Benchmarks and Aggregated Data

We may use your diagnostic data — including your Nexus Score and identified gaps — in anonymized, aggregated form to create industry benchmarks, research reports, and product improvements. Aggregated data contains no personally identifiable information and cannot reasonably be used to identify you or your business.

8. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time (via unsubscribe link or by emailing us)
  • Withdraw consent for SMS or voice communications at any time

To submit a deletion request or exercise any of these rights, email us at zach@nexuszc.com. We will honor all deletion requests within 30 days. Note that we may be required to retain certain records for legal or accounting purposes even after deletion.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use minimal session cookies for functional purposes (e.g., preventing duplicate form submissions). We do not currently use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. We may use basic analytics (page view counts, form completion rates) using self-hosted or privacy-respecting tools.

10. Security

We implement industry-standard security practices including encrypted data transmission (TLS), encrypted database storage, access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If a breach occurs that affects your data, we will notify you promptly.

11. Children

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be communicated to active clients via email. Continued use of our Services after changes constitutes acceptance.
