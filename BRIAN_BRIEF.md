# Brian Brief — Cash-Out Refi Deal

## Context
- Referred by Jesse and Kevin
- Brian reaching out this week

- This is our anchor client and proof case for the VA + System product

---

## What We're Selling Brian

**Three VAs (best English speakers) + Lead Follow-up & CRM System**

- 3 dedicated VAs, 40 hrs/week each, calling foreclosure list
- Lead Follow-up & CRM System runs behind them: tracks every call, every connect, every callback, every outcome — so leads don't slip
- Sam (our COO) does a weekly 15-min review call to keep quality high
- Kristine handles VA onboarding, training, payroll, swaps if anyone isn't fit

---

**$3,050/mo total**
- 3 VAs at $850/mo each = $2,550/mo

- Total: $3,050/mo
- Deploy timeline: VAs picked, trained, AND v1 of the CRM system built within 7 days of go-ahead

**If he asks for more or fewer VAs:**

## Discovery Questions (Walk Him Through These)

1. **Scope** — "How many VAs are you imagining? 2, 3, 4?"
2. **Volume** — "Walk me through what you want them doing — how many calls per day per person? What's the typical sequence after a connect?"
3. **Tools** — "What CRM are you currently using, if any? Where's the lead list — spreadsheet, Salesforce, somewhere else?"
4. **Timeline** — "When do you want them live? Is the foreclosure list time-sensitive?"
5. **Success metric** — "What does 'this is working' look like to you in week 2? In month 2?"
6. **Failure handling** — "If a specific VA isn't a fit, how do you want to handle it? Easy swap, or a more formal review?"

## What "Lead Follow-up & CRM System" Means (If He Asks)

Plain language for Brian:
- Every lead that gets called is logged with outcome (connected, voicemail, callback, rejected)
- Every callback request is automatically scheduled and assigned to a VA
- Every "almost interested" lead enters a follow-up sequence so we don't lose them
- Weekly report shows: calls made, connects, callbacks scheduled, leads in pipeline, leads converted
- He gets a dashboard he can check anytime to see what's happening with his list

This is the difference between "we hired some callers" and "we built you a calling operation."
