## Q4: What "self-improving" means

What does "24/7 self-improving brain" actually mean to me?

(a) Mechanical improvement — better classifications, better retrieval, automatic
(b) Knowledge accumulation — gets smarter about each business as data flows in
(c) Strategic learning — notices patterns in advice that worked vs ignored, adapts prompts
(d) Autonomous action — does things in the world without per-action approval

**My answer:**
Semi-autonomous initially — action requires my approval until a workflow has proven reliable. Then graduate to fully autonomous on those proven workflows. The brain works 24/7 on safe operations (re-classifications, assessments, self-improvement of its own prompts) without approval.

## Operating Principles

(For Claude Code and Claude.ai to follow when working on Nexus)

- This is Zach's personal Chief of Staff system, evolving toward a productizable autonomous business operations engine
- The platform is Nexus + VA company. Verticals (Roofing OS, Cash Out Refinances) are GTM channels built on top
- Build order: VA orchestration → outreach engine (on top of VA orchestration) → CRM → website generator (productized add-on)
- Revenue model: productized service or done-for-you ops (or hybrid) — not pure SaaS
- Target: 3 paying clients within 90 days covering costs + Zach's time
- Cost discipline: use cheaper models (Haiku) for classification/retrieval, premium (Sonnet/Opus) only for high-leverage generation tasks
- Autonomy model: semi-autonomous first, graduate proven workflows to fully autonomous; safe background ops (re-classifications, assessments, prompt self-improvement) run 24/7 without approval
- Single-user now, multi-tenant is v2 — don't over-engineer for scale prematurely

## Resolved Tensions

- **Speed vs autonomy**: 90-day client target requires shipping working systems fast. Fully autonomous action is too risky in that window. Resolution: ship semi-autonomous workflows to clients, graduate to autonomous as trust accrues.
- **Outreach difficulty vs lead gen value**: Outreach is highest leverage but hardest to harden in 90 days. Resolution: VA orchestration is the foundation; outreach is built ON TOP of it as a workflow type, not as the core product.
- **Always-on vs cost discipline**: 24/7 brain operations cost money. Resolution: cheap background operations (re-classifications, accumulation) run constantly. Premium model calls (strategic synthesis, COO assessments) run on-demand or scheduled.
