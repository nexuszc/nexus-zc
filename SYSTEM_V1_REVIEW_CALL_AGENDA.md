# Weekly Review Call Agenda — 15 Minutes

**Run by:** Sam
**Cadence:** Every week, same day/time
**Format:** Video or phone, client + Sam only

---

## Before the Call (Sam Prep — 5 min)

- Pull the weekly report for this client
- Note any blockers from the task tracker
- Know the hours used vs allocated
- Have 1 specific win and 1 specific improvement ready

---

## Agenda

### 1. Last Week's Wins — 2 min

Open with what went well. Keep energy up.

**Sam's opening:** "Before we get into anything, I want to share what the team knocked out this week..."

**Suggested questions:**
- "Did anything stand out to you from the report?"
- "Were there any moments where you felt the team was really on it?"

---

### 2. This Week's Focus — 3 min

Align on the top 2-3 priorities for the coming week before anything else.

**Sam's framing:** "Here's what I'm planning to have the team focused on this week — does that match what's most important to you right now?"

**Suggested questions:**
- "Is there anything time-sensitive this week I should know about?"
- "Any leads or accounts you want prioritized?"
- "Anything shifting on your end that changes what we should be working on?"

---

### 3. Blockers / Questions — 5 min

Work through anything stuck. This is the most important part of the call.

**Sam's framing:** "We had [X] items flagged as blocked this week — let me walk through them quickly..."

**Suggested questions:**
- "Is there anything in the tracker or report that confused you or raised a question?"
- "Is there anything the team is waiting on from your side?"
- "Any leads or situations where you weren't sure how we handled it?"

---

### 4. Adjustments Needed — 3 min

Make real-time decisions on anything that needs to change.

**Suggested questions:**
- "Is the current pace/volume right, or do you want us to push harder on anything?"
- "Is the reporting giving you what you need, or is there something you wish you could see?"
- "Any feedback on the VAs — communication style, responsiveness, accuracy?"

**If they raise a VA quality issue:** "Got it — I'll address that directly this week and follow up with you by [day]."

---

### 5. Quick Admin — 2 min

Handle logistics before hanging up.

**Standing checklist:**
- Confirm same time next week
- Any upcoming dates to flag (client travel, holidays, list deliveries)
- Anything Sam needs to send before end of week

**Sam's close:** "Great — I'll get the team updated on priorities today. You'll have next Friday's report in your inbox by 5pm. Talk soon."
