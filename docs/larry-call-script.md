# Larry Call Script — Roofing OS Demo Call
## Zero-Touch Onboarding Model (updated Phase 9)

---

## BEFORE THE CALL

- Pull up `app.nexuszc.com/roofing/contractors` — confirm Larry is in the pipeline or just signed up
- Have `roofingos.dev/demo` open in a second tab — you'll walk him through the demo portal live
- Know his CRM (AccuLynx / JobNimbus / paper) — ask if you don't know

---

## OPENING (0:00–1:00)

> "Hey Larry, this is Zach — I sent you a message about Roofing OS. Got two minutes?
> I'm not gonna pitch you. I just want to show you something you've never seen before
> and get your honest reaction. Sound fair?"

**If yes:** "Perfect. Pull up your phone or a browser — I'm going to send you a link right now."

*[Send roofingos.dev/demo via text or email while on the call]*

---

## DEMO WALK (1:00–5:00)

Walk him through the demo portal — he's the homeowner's contractor:

1. **Stage bar** — "See those five stages? Homeowner sees exactly where their job is, 24/7. No more 'where's my crew' calls."

2. **Activity feed** — "Every update you log — crew arriving, materials dropped, install complete — posts here automatically. Bilingual. English and Spanish."

3. **Insurance claim section** — "If it's an insurance job, the adjuster info, claim number, supplement status — all visible to the homeowner. They feel informed. You look professional."

4. **Aria bubble** — "See that chat button? Homeowner texts Aria — our AI — at 2am asking about their deductible. Aria answers. You sleep."

5. **Documents tab** — "Contract, permit, warranty — all here. They sign digitally. You don't chase paper."

**Pause.** "What's your reaction so far?"

---

## THE PIVOT (5:00–6:00)

> "Here's the thing — this is free. Forever. No card. You create an account, add a job,
> and your homeowner gets a portal link in under 5 minutes. That's it.
>
> The free plan covers unlimited jobs and the homeowner portal.
> When you're ready for Aria doing your follow-up calls, or AI supplement writing,
> those are add-ons. But you don't need any of that to get started today."

---

## ZERO-TOUCH SIGNUP FLOW

> "Here's how it works — I'm not going to set this up for you or make you get on a Zoom.
> You go to roofingos.dev, click 'Create my free account', enter your email.
> You'll get a magic link — no password, no card.
> You're inside in 30 seconds. Add your first job, we walk you through it."

**If he asks about setup help:**
> "Our onboarding is built into the dashboard — it walks you through adding your first job
> step by step. If you get stuck, there's a chat button and I'm personally responsive within the hour."

---

## OBJECTIONS

**"I already use AccuLynx / JobNimbus."**
> "Good — we connect directly. You don't replace it. Your jobs pull in automatically.
> Roofing OS is the homeowner-facing layer that sits on top — the portal, the AI,
> the automated follow-up. They do the back office. We do the customer experience."

**"What does it cost?"**
> "Zero to start. Portal is free forever. The add-ons are:
> - Measurements (aerial reports): $49/mo
> - Aria (AI follow-up calls): $149/mo
> - Supplement AI: $499/mo
> - All In bundle: $1,299/mo
>
> Most guys start free, add Aria after they see the portal working."

**"How is this different from XactAnalysis / EagleView?"**
> "EagleView is measurements only — $25-40 per report. We're $49/month unlimited.
> XactAnalysis is carrier-side. We're contractor-side. Totally different layer."

**"I'm busy — not a good time to try something new."**
> "That's exactly why this is built the way it is. You sign up in 30 seconds, add one job,
> send your next homeowner the portal link. If it doesn't save you time in the first week,
> you haven't lost anything. There's no contract, no card, no setup call required."
