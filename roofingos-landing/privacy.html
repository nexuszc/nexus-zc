By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive SMS messages from Roofing OS / Nexus ZC LLC. These messages may include account alerts, job status updates, and promotional messages about Roofing OS. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

How to opt out: Reply STOP to any SMS message at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent. You can also email zach@roofingos.dev to request removal.

How to get help: Reply HELP to any SMS message or email zach@roofingos.dev.

We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for their own marketing purposes. Your phone number is used solely to send you messages related to Roofing OS and your account.
