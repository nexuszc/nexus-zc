How Hippo Handles Roof Claims

Hippo uses a hybrid model combining digital inspections with occasional in-person adjuster visits. Hippo's algorithms flag inconsistent damage presentations. Second, get your scope right the first time. Hippo's adjusters cross-reference local wind speeds and typical damage patterns. Common Contractor Mistakes With Hippo

The biggest mistake is over-scoping damage. Hippo's software detects pattern inconsistencies better than most carriers. Second, contractors often fail to address insurance requirements in their initial estimates.
