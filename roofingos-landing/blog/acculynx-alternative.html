AccuLynx's published pricing starts at $85–$95 per user per month with a minimum user count. A 6-person team — you, a PM, two sales reps, an office manager, and a field supervisor — runs $550–$600/month on the base plan before you add any premium modules. Larger teams hit $1,200/month easily.

JobNimbus starts at $619/month for smaller teams under its standard workflow package. Power users with automation, document management, and reporting add-ons push past $900/month.

Both require annual contracts. If you sign up and realize the software doesn't fit your workflow, you're paying for it anyway.

The Per-User Fee Problem

Per-user pricing punishes growth. Every time you hire, your software bill goes up. You have a sales rep who only logs in twice a week to update job status — you're paying full price for that seat. You bring on a second PM during storm season — that's another $85–$100/month. By the time you have 8 people who need CRM access, you're paying $700–$800/month whether those users are active daily or not.

Roofing OS CRM is $299/month. That's the price for your entire team. Add users, don't add users, hire more sales reps during storm season — the bill doesn't change.
