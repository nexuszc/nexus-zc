Questions

How does the supplement AI actually work?▼
You upload photos of the job and the adjuster's estimate. Our AI analyzes every photo for damage types and cross-references them against the adjuster's line items. It identifies what was missed, checks Colorado building codes, and generates a complete supplement package in the carrier's preferred language — all in under 10 minutes.
Does the homeowner need to download an app?▼
No. The homeowner gets a text with a link. They tap it and their portal opens in their browser — no app download, no account creation, no password. It works on any phone. Most homeowners open it within 5 minutes of receiving the text.
What carriers does the supplement AI support?▼
State Farm, Allstate, Liberty Mutual, Travelers, USAA, Nationwide, and Farmers are fully supported with carrier-specific language, known denial tactics, and rebuttal strategies. All other carriers get our standard supplement format.
What is Aria and can she really make calls?▼
Aria is an AI assistant that handles phone calls, texts, and chat. She's always disclosed as AI. She calls previous customers when a storm hits their area, captures inbound leads after hours, follows up with homeowners, and communicates with adjusters. She gets smarter every week from call outcomes.
How quickly will I see ROI?▼
Most contractors recover their first supplement within the first job they run through Roofing OS. At $4,200 average supplement recovery and a $299/month subscription, one recovered supplement pays for 14 months of the platform.
Is my homeowner's data secure?▼
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Homeowner portals are access-controlled with unique one-time tokens. We never sell or share homeowner data. The platform is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure.
