Questions

Do my homeowners need to download an app?

No. They get a text with a link. They tap it and their portal opens in their browser — no account, no password, no app download. It works on any phone. Most homeowners open it within 5 minutes of the first text.

What is Aria?

Aria is an AI assistant built for roofing. At the $49/month tier, she handles text chat inside the homeowner portal — answering questions about job status, insurance, documents, and next steps 24/7. She's always disclosed as AI. She gets smarter every week from real conversations.

How does the 14-day trial work?

You enter your card but we don't charge it for 14 days. Run it on real jobs. If you don't love it, cancel before day 14 and owe nothing. After that it's $49/month, billed monthly, cancel anytime with no penalty.

Can I upgrade to the full AI supplement and voice system later?

Yes. When you're ready for AI-powered supplement documentation, Aria outbound voice calls, storm detection, and full job automation — the upgrade is one click from your dashboard. Most contractors start at $49 and upgrade after seeing their first supplement recovered.
