FAQ

Quick answers.

How long does setup take?

5 minutes. Create your account, create your first job, send the link to your homeowner. That's it. No training. No onboarding calls. No learning curve. If you can text, you can use Roofing OS.

Does my homeowner need to download an app?

No. It's a link. Works on any phone, any browser. They tap it and they're in. No account. No password. No app store. Most homeowners open it within 5 minutes of the first text.

Do I have to update it manually?

No. Connect your existing tools — CompanyCam, AccuLynx, JobNimbus — and the portal updates automatically. Or update it yourself in 10 seconds from your phone. Either way, your homeowner stays informed.

What is Aria?

Aria is an AI assistant built for roofing. She answers homeowner questions 24/7 inside the portal — insurance status, job timeline, what's happening and why. She's always disclosed as AI. She gets smarter every week from real conversations.

How much does it cost?

The portal is free forever. No card required. Unlimited jobs, unlimited homeowners. Add-ons (Aria AI, Supplement AI, Measurements) are available when you're ready — starting at $49/mo. The portal pays for itself the first time a homeowner stops calling during an installation.
