Yes. Portal Pro is free for up to 5 jobs/month. No credit card required, no trial period, no bait-and-switch. Paid plans start at $69/month for unlimited jobs when you're ready to scale.

Do my homeowners need to download an app?

No. They get a link via text or email. It opens in any browser on any phone — iPhone, Android, doesn't matter. No account, no password, no app store. Most homeowners open it within 5 minutes of receiving the link.

What happens to my CompanyCam photos?

We import them automatically. Paste your CompanyCam API key and all your existing projects and photos sync into Roofing OS in minutes. Your photos, your data — available forever even if you cancel CompanyCam.

Does this work with AccuLynx or JobNimbus?

Yes — we integrate with both, plus CompanyCam, EagleView, and four other tools. Connect your existing stack and the portal stays updated automatically. No manual entry required.

How long does setup take?

4 minutes. We timed it. Create your account, create your first job, send the link to your homeowner. That's it. No training calls, no onboarding sessions, no learning curve. If you can send a text, you can use Roofing OS.
