Acculynx: What It Does and Who It's For

Acculynx has been around for years and has earned its reputation in the roofing industry. It's a purpose-built roofing CRM that handles everything from lead tracking and job management to material ordering and production scheduling. If you're running a mid-to-large roofing operation with an established team, Acculynx gives you a solid, dependable backbone.

The platform integrates with suppliers like ABC Supply and Beacon, which is genuinely useful for companies doing high volume. It also handles insurance claim tracking, photo documentation, and crew scheduling reasonably well. Their mobile app works in the field and most roofing veterans are already familiar with the interface.

There's also no free plan — you're committing to a paid subscription from day one. For a solo contractor or a company just scaling up, that's a real barrier.
