Roofing OS vs JobNimbus: Which Is Better for Roofing Contractors in 2026?

Quick Answer: JobNimbus is a solid, established CRM that works well for larger roofing companies with big budgets and complex sales pipelines. Roofing OS is built specifically for insurance-focused roofing contractors who want more automation, a homeowner portal, and AI-assisted supplementing — starting free instead of $550/month.

JobNimbus: What It Does and Who It's For

JobNimbus has been around since 2013 and has earned a real reputation in the roofing industry. It's a CRM and project management platform that helps roofing companies track leads, manage jobs, send estimates, and coordinate their teams. If you've been in roofing for a few years, you've probably heard of it — and for good reason.

The platform covers the basics well: contact management, job boards, document storage, reporting dashboards, and integrations with tools like EagleView and Hover. Their mobile app is functional, and their customer support has improved over the years.

Where JobNimbus shines is for established companies with a dedicated office staff, a sales team managing high lead volume, and the operational bandwidth to configure and customize a fairly complex system. It's not a lightweight tool — it's a full CRM platform, and it's priced accordingly.

Pricing: JobNimbus starts at approximately $550/month for their CRM plan. For smaller teams or contractors just scaling up, that's a significant monthly commitment before you've proven ROI.

The learning curve is real. Many contractors report spending weeks getting the system fully set up, and some features — like automation workflows — require technical comfort to configure properly. It's powerful, but it rewards patience and resources.

Roofing OS: What It Does and Who It's For

I built Roofing OS because I kept seeing the same problem: roofing contractors — especially insurance restoration contractors — were either using generic CRMs that didn't understand their workflow, or they were paying enterprise prices for tools with features they'd never use.

When to Choose JobNimbus

I want to be straight with you here — JobNimbus is a legitimate platform and there are real situations where it's the right call:

  • You run a large, multi-division roofing company with a dedicated operations manager who can invest time in configuration and training. JobNimbus rewards that investment.
  • You have a high-volume retail or commercial sales process that needs deep CRM customization, advanced reporting, and integration with a wider tech stack.
  • Your team is already trained on it. Switching costs are real. If your crew knows JobNimbus and it's working reasonably well, the disruption of switching might not be worth it unless you have clear pain points.

When to Choose Roofing OS
